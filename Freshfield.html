Freshfields Village

Nestled at the meeting point of John's Island, Seabrook Island, and Kiawa Island, Freshfields Village proves the perfect place to spend your evenings. Equipped with numerous both preppy and relaxed restaurants, ice cream shops, boutiques, and bands, the coastal area can be driven to in a golf cart and is where all the teens come to hang out! Coupled with its sophisticated atmosphere comes Freshfields Village's pet friendliness, as there are dog houses lining the village where dogs can step into and cool off in the air conditioning!
